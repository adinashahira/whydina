Why Dina?

Strengths

Commitment

My dedication in designing and coding beautiful websites exceeds my dedication to run my Sims family through 10 generations. Despite investing my money on buying game packs, I still found myself sitting down in front of my laptop, searching up for inspiration on Dribble, and crafting an inspired personal project. As cliche as it may be, I would say programming is my hobby. I am a 100% committed to keep on levelling up my skills and become a webmaster Yoda.

Obsessed with Planning

I am in love with everything about planning. I am obsessed with dotting every 'i's and crossing every 't's. My obsession can be seen when I used to take away 3 hours to create a syllabus just for self learning and structuring my plan to the deepest of its core. You bet I have my own design system just to make this website. What is this obsession gonna bring to the table? Perfectionism in every task that I do!


Weaknesses

Lack of Experience

I never actually coded anything for any big companies (or coded anything useful lol). That means that I will step into the room full of developers with the littlest amount of skills on my hand. If you think that's a showstopper, take a step back, and watch me do my thing. Watch me continuously learning and watch me prove my worth because that's how dedicated I am. My dessert might be lacking of sweetness today, but tomorrow, you'll find yourself craving more for it *wink*.


Goals

To be the Master...

I believe that everyone must start somewhere from the bottom before the reached the peak of their success so it's okay to start from nothing and grow into something. With this opportunity given to me, to be as a part of the development team in Etiqa, I will one day reach my dream of becoming a master web developer. Now you may see that I'm on the ground, later you'll see me holding the universe :D
